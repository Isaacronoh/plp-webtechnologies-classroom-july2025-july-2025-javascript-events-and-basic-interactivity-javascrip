WAJIR EAST TVC

Wajir East TVC is a technical institution based in Waqberi, Wajir County, Kenya. It was established in 2023 and has since become a popular technical institution in the region. The institution offers various technical courses, including electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and information technology. Wajir East TVC is committed to providing quality education and training to its students, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers.
